Helpful Links for Reading “Welcome to the Anthropocene”

September 23, 2015 DrSexton

Hello all,

Your reading for Friday is an excerpt from Elizabeth Kolbert’s book The Sixth Extinction, a chapter called “Welcome to the Anthropocene.” Since we are only reading the chapter introducing the idea of the anthropocene, we are not reading any of the parts of the book where she introduces helpful geological terminology – specifically, the “Big Five” extinction events predating the extinction event arguably taking place in the present day.
The attached BBC Nature website provides a quick, efficient reference to the “big five” extinctions that Kolbert mentions. You’ll definitely see her referring to the Ordovician-Silurian, in particular. Also, I’m attaching a link to a graphic that provides a quick, basic overview of terms demarking geologic time, which Kolbert also uses.
BBC Nature: http://www.bbc.co.uk/nature/extinction_events
Graphic: http://static.nautil.us/2439_fc79250f8c5b804390e8da280b4cf06e.png
See you Friday!
Dr. Sexton
